Postscript.

The American editor omitted in the proper place (p. 315, note 4, after what is said by the translator) to insert this important note: viz.,-

[On this matter of quotations from anonymous authors of the apostolic times, not infrequently made by Irenaeus, consult the importatnt tractate of Dr. Routh, in his Reliquiae Sacrae, vol. i. 45-68.]
